Information about Depression:

Depression is a mental health disorder characterized by persistent feelings of sadness and a lack of interest or pleasure in daily activities.

Precautions and Measures:

  • Build a support system.
  • Engage in regular physical activity.
  • Practice self-care.
  • Establish a daily routine.
  • Avoid excessive alcohol and substance use.
  • Consider medication if prescribed.
  • Attend therapy sessions consistently.
  • Monitor and challenge negative thoughts.
  • Educate yourself and loved ones about depression.

{% elif result == "ADHD" %}

Information about ADHD:

ADHD is a neurodevelopmental disorder marked by inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity.

Precautions and Measures:

  • Establish routines and structure.
  • Break tasks into smaller steps.
  • Use visual aids and reminders.
  • Encourage regular physical activity.
  • Explore behavioral therapy.
  • Consider medication if prescribed.
  • Provide a quiet and organized workspace.
  • Encourage healthy sleep habits.
  • Teach and reinforce social skills.
  • Involve family and support networks.
  • Stay informed and seek guidance.
{% elif result == "Diabetes, Type 2" %}

Information about Diabetes, Type 2:

A chronic condition affecting how your body processes sugar due to insulin resistance or deficiency.

Precautions and Measures:

  • Adopt a balanced diet.
  • Maintain a healthy weight.
  • Engage in regular physical activity.
  • Monitor blood sugar levels.
  • Take medications as prescribed.
  • Manage stress and stay hydrated.
  • Quit smoking.
  • Regular check-ups and foot care.
  • Limit alcohol and sugar intake.
{% elif result == "High Blood Pressure" %}

Information about High Blood Pressure:

A condition where blood pressure in the arteries is consistently elevated, increasing risk of heart disease.

Precautions and Measures:

  • Eat a heart-healthy diet.
  • Maintain a healthy weight.
  • Exercise regularly.
  • Limit alcohol and caffeine.
  • Quit smoking and manage stress.
  • Monitor blood pressure regularly.
  • Take medications as prescribed.
  • Ensure quality sleep.
